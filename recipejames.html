Pilau Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, dicked
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon tumeric
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and saute onions and garlic until golden brown.
  2. Add diced chicken and brown on all sides.
  3. Stir in cumin, coriander, and tumeric until well combined.
  4. Add rice, chicken broth and coconut milk. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  6. Fluff the pilau with a fork and let it sit covered for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious Pilau Chicken!
